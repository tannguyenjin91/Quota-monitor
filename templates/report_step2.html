How to assign fields

Use one row field and one column field for the simplest cross-tab. Add quota fields for grouped banner columns.
  • Row: creates the table rows.
  • Column: creates the table columns.
  • Quota: creates grouped quota/banner columns.
  • Ignore: keeps the field out of the final output.
